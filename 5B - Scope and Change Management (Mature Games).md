This track focuses on managing scope and change in running live operations where the game is already launched and generating revenue.

# 5B.1 Live Ops Scope Creep Audit

## Why It Matters

In live games, scope creep happens in small increments across every event, every content drop, every sale. The monthly event that used to take 2 days now takes 5.
