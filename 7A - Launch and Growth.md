# 7A.5 Soft Launch Market Selection

## Why It Matters

Soft launch is your final validation before committing global UA spend. The wrong market can give you misleading dataâ€”either too optimistic (cheap users who don't monetize) or too pessimistic (expensive users who aren't your target). Market selection directly impacts the quality of your go/no-go decision.
