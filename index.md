## üìù About This Playbook

This workbook provides frameworks, checklists, and templates for game development teams. It's designed to be:

- **Adaptable** ‚Äî Take what works, customize what's close, ignore what doesn't fit
- **Practical** ‚Äî Focused on real problems teams face, not theoretical best practices
- **Technology Agnostic** ‚Äî Principles apply across engines, platforms, and genres
- **Living** ‚Äî Meant to evolve as your team and game evolve

The goal isn't to follow this workbook slavishly‚Äîit's to use it as raw material to build your own studio playbook.
